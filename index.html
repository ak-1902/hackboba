About Boba

Boba, also known as bubble tea, originated in Taiwan in the 1980s. It’s a drink with tea and tapioca pearls and many flavors.

Flavors

  • Classic Milk Tea
  • Taro
  • Matcha
  • Mango
  • Strawberry

Easy Boba Recipes

Homemade pearls

  1. Mix 1 cup of tapioca starch with ½ cup of boiling water.
  2. Knead until a dough forms, then roll into small balls.
  3. Boil the pearls for about 20 minutes, then let them cool.
